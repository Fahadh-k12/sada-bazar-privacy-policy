Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I place an order?

Download the Sada Bazar app, browse products, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can pay with Cash on Delivery!

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges vary based on your location and order value. Orders above a certain amount may qualify for free delivery.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order before it's dispatched. Contact our support team immediately for assistance.

What payment methods do you accept?

Currently, we accept Cash on Delivery (COD). You pay when you receive your order at your doorstep.

How long does delivery take?

Delivery typically takes 1-2 days depending on your location. You'll receive updates on your order status.

What if I receive damaged products?

Contact us immediately with photos of the damaged items. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.
